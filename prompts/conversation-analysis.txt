The conversation transcript may be in any of these languages:
Arabic (ar), German (de), Greek (el), English (en), Finnish (fi),
French (fr), Italian (it), Norwegian (no), or Portuguese (pt).
Detect the language of the conversation and return it as an ISO 639-1 code in the "language" field.
ALL other output fields (summary, agent_performance_notes, recommended_action, key_quotes, alert_reason, etc.)
must be written in English regardless of the conversation language.

CONVERSATION DATA:
Conversation ID: {{conversation_id}}

SEVERITY SCALE (dissatisfaction_severity):
- "Low"      — Minor frustration, complaint fully resolved during chat, player tone normalized by end
- "Medium"   — Clear dissatisfaction, issue partially resolved or player still uneasy at conversation end
- "High"     — Strong dissatisfaction, issue not resolved, player may churn or escalate
- "Critical" — Any of: legal/regulatory threat ("I'll report you", "lawyer", "gaming authority"), VIP/high-value player complaint, repeated abuse patterns, potential fraud indicators, safety risk, inappropriate agent conduct

ISSUE CATEGORIES (issue_category — pick exactly one):
- "Payment/Withdrawal"
- "Game Bug"
- "Login/Account"
- "Bonus/Promotion"
- "Technical Error"
- "Website & Navigation"
- "Device & Compatibility"
- "Slow Response"
- "Inappropriate Communication"
- "Other"

RESOLUTION STATUS (resolution_status):
IMPORTANT: Base this on what the player says and feels at the END of the conversation — NOT on whether Intercom marks the conversation as "closed". A closed conversation where the player is still angry = "Unresolved".
- "Resolved"           — Player explicitly confirms issue is fixed, thanks agent, tone is positive
- "Partially Resolved" — Some progress made but player still has concerns or open questions
- "Unresolved"         — Issue not addressed, player still frustrated, or conversation ended abruptly without resolution

AGENT PERFORMANCE SCORE (agent_performance_score — integer 1-5, or null):
- If Is Bot Handled is true: set agent_performance_score to null and agent_performance_notes to "N/A — conversation handled by bot"
- 5: Exceptional — Empathetic, clear, fully resolved issue efficiently, personalized response
- 4: Good — Professional, resolved issue with minor gaps in communication
- 3: Adequate — Issue resolved but communication was robotic, slow, or unclear
- 2: Below Standard — Repetitive scripted responses, failed to resolve, frustrated player further
- 1: Poor — Rude, inappropriate, harmful, or dishonest communication

ALERT WORTHY (is_alert_worthy):
Set to true if ANY of the following are present:
- Player mentions legal action, gaming authority, regulator, lawyer, or public complaint
- Player is a confirmed VIP or high-value account (look for indicators in conversation)
- Player has submitted 3+ recent complaints (if mentioned in conversation context)
- Agent used inappropriate, rude, or potentially discriminatory language
- Conversation contains fraud indicators or safety concerns

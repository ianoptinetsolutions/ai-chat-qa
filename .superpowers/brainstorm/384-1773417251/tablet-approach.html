How should the dashboard behave on tablet?

Mobile (< 768px) will always use a bottom nav bar. Choose how tablet (768px–1279px) behaves.

A

Icon-only sidebar on tablet

The sidebar collapses to a slim 60px rail showing just icons. Hover/tap reveals labels as tooltips. Full 220px sidebar on desktop (1280px+).

Pros

  • Always visible nav
  • Efficient use of space
  • Familiar desktop pattern

Cons

  • Icons alone may confuse
  • Less touch-friendly
B

Hamburger menu on tablet

Top bar with hamburger icon. Tapping slides in a full-width overlay sidebar. Nav hidden until opened. Full sidebar on desktop (1280px+).

CHAT QA

Pros

  • Maximum content space
  • Touch-friendly when open

Cons

  • Hidden nav (discoverability)
  • Extra tap required
C

Bottom nav on tablet too

Use the same bottom nav bar as mobile on both mobile and tablet (up to 1279px). Full sidebar only on large desktop.

Pros

  • Consistent mobile + tablet UX
  • Modern app feel
  • Touch-optimized

Cons

  • Less space-efficient on wider tablets
